# Pan Broccoli

## Ingredients

* broccoli florets
* chili oil
* 1 - 2 tbsp water

## Directions

1. cold sear, aka start with cold pan
2. good amount of oil (tiny fry), broccoli, water
3. cover, high heat to evaporate water
4. 4 minutes
5. flip, salt, repeat uncovered
